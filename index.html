Olympics

The Olympic Games are the world's biggest international sports competition.
Athletes from many countries participate to represent nations and compete
for gold, silver, and bronze medals. The Olympics are held every four years and include
many sports such as athletics, football, swimming, gymnastics, and basketball. The main
purpose of the Olympic Games is to promote peace, friendship, unity, and fair competition
among people from different cultures. Winning an Olympic medal is considered one of the
greatest achievements for any athlete.
