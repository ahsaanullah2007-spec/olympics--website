Introduction Olympic Games

The Olympic Games are the world's largest international sports event.

Olympic Motto

Faster, Higher, Stronger - Together

The motto encourages athletes to perform their best while working together.

Olympic Values

    Excellence - Giving your best in sports.
    Friendship - understanding among people.
    Respect - Respecting rules, opponents, and fair play.

Olympic Rings

The Olympic symbolized consists of five rings:

  • Blue
  • Yellow
  • Black
  • Green
  • Red

These rings represent the unity of the five inhabited continents.
