About Olympics

The Olympic Games are the world's largest international multi-sport event, bringing together athletes from around the world. They are held every four years and promote excellence, friendship, and respect among nations. The Olympics inspire millions of people through sports, unity, determination, and fair competition. Athletes compete in a wide variety of sports while representing their countries with pride.
